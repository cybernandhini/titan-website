What is Titan?

Titan IoT Authenticator is a secure, scalable, and role-based authentication system designed for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) operating in IoT-enabled and traditional environments. Developed under Cyber Nandhini, Titan addresses the growing need for affordable cybersecurity in SME infrastructures.
